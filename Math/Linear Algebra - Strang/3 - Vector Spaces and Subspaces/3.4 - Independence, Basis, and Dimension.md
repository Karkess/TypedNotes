## 3.4 - Independence, Basis, and Dimension

1. **Independent Columns of $A$: The only solution to $A\vec{x}=\vec{0}$ is $\vec{x}=\vec{0}$. The nullspace is $\mathbb{Z}$.
2. Independent vectors: The only zero combination $c_1v_1+\dots+c_kv_k=0$ has all $c=0$. 
3. A matrix with $m<n$ has **dependent columns**: At least $n-m$ free variables/special solutions.
4. The vectors $v_1,\dots,v_k$ **span the space** $\mathbb{S}$ if $\mathbb{S}=$ all combinations of $\vec{v}$'s'. 
5. The vectors $\vec{v_1},\dots,\vec{v_k}$ are a **basis** for $\mathbb{S}$ if they are independent and they span $\mathbb{S}$. 
6. The **dimension of a space** $\mathbb{S}$ is the number of vectors in every basis for $\mathbb{S}$. 
7. If $A$ is $4\times4$ and invertible, its columns are a basis for $\mathbb{R}^4$. The dimension of $\mathbb{R}^4$ is $4$.
