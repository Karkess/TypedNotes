### Lecture 5 Start
Fundamental Theorems of the Gradient, Divergence, and Curl
$$\text{Gradient Theorem:}\quad\oint^b_a(\nabla T)\cdot\,d\vec{\ell}=T(b)-T(a)$$$$\text{Divergence Theorem:}\quad\int_\mathcal{V}(\vec{\nabla}\cdot\vec{T})\cdot\,dS=\oint_\mathcal{S}\vec{T}\cdot\,d\vec{A}$$$$\text{Curl Theorem:}\quad\int_\mathcal{S}[\vec{\nabla}\times\vec{v}]\cdot\,d\vec{S}=\oint_\ell\vec{v}\cdot\,d\vec{\ell}$$
